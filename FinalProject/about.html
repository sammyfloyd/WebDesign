About the Artist

Sam Floyd

Hello there! I am a junior college student currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in Media Arts. I attend the University of Montana, in Missoula, and my goal is to get the tools and skillset to one day be a concept artist for a movie, video game, or show. A couple of my biggest inspirations are Smiling Friends, FFXIV, Outlast Trials, Star Wars, and Little Nightmares! I am using this website as a way to post about my artworks, doodles and all!
