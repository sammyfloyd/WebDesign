About the Author

Bryan Lee O'Malley

Personal Life

Bryan Lee O'Malley was born February 21, 1979 in London, Ontario, Canada. He briefly went to the University of Western Ontario majoring in Film Studies, but dropped out before deciding to commit himself to becoming a cartoonist. He went on to help illustrate and letter a number of Oni Press comics, then released his first original graphic novel titled Lost at Sea, released in 2003. After this, he worked on and became famous for the Scott Pilgrim series and is still working on graphic novels to this day.
