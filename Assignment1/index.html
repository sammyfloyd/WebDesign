Book Introduction

Scott Pilgrim is a 23-year-old slacker living in Toronto. He plays bass in a rock band called Sex Bob-Omb. Currently, heâ€™s between jobs and dating a 17-year-old high school girl, Knives Chau. His life seems smooth until he starts dreaming about a mysterious delivery girl named Ramona Flowers. She rollerblades into his dreams and catches him off-guard at parties. Little does Scott know that pursuing Ramona will send his life spiraling into chaos and adventure.

Chapter 1: Dating a High Schooler

Scott Pilgrim, the members of his band, and his gay roommate, Wallace, are introduced. Scott (bass), Stephen (vocals and guitar), Kim (drums), and Young Neil (biggest fan and also bass), make up Sex Bob-omb, a basement indie rock band. Everything seems peachy until it is revealed that Scott Pilgrim is dating Knives Chau, a high schooler! They're relationship seems innocent but Scott gets criticized by his surrounding friends and falls asleep one night to the girl of his dreams rollerblading past him.

Chapter 2: Do You Know Her?

Scott Pilgrim wakes up the next morning, only to encounter this "dream girl" on his trip to the library with his high school girlfriend. Shocked, he asks around if anyone knows her at a party he attends and discovers her name is Ramona Flowers. Shortly afterwards, he encounters her there.

Chapter 3: This One Girl...

He compliments her shoes, asks her if he's dreaming, then stalks her until she leaves the party... Scott finds out from other people that she's an American that moved there recently and got a job at Amazon, and that she recently had a breakup! Scott goes home to order a package to encounter Ramona once again and is suprised when Knives shows up at the door, you know, his underage girlfriend. She kisses him and Scott has another dream of Ramona zooming by.
